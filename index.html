Custom truck painting in the UK

Expert custom truck company

An expert custom truck painting service can paint every make and model of truck, up to the largest commercial vehicles. They will paint both new and old trucks, and offer services from straightforward panel painting to full body resprays.

From the smallest touch-up to the most extensive paint job, the best custom truck painting services offer every type of paint finish and use first-rate products, using PPG and Glasurit computerised paint mixing systems to expertly match paint manufacturers’ swatches. These tools match colours accurately which—together with a dust-free environment —will produce a finish which matches even the most stringent standards.

A professional custom truck painting service will be Office of Fair Trading Approved and EPA registered, and have the latest facilities and skilled technicians.

If you have a single truck or even a fleet of vehicles which need custom painting, you should choose an experienced and professional reliable custom truck painting company. However, what should you look for?

Commercial Colours

We asked the owner of UK-based commercial vehicle accident, repair, and painting centre Commercial Colours, Mark Jaques, about how to select the best custom truck painting company, and he told us:
